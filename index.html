Welcome to the World of Ecosystems

An ecosystem is a community of living organisms interacting with each other and their non-living environment. From small ponds to vast rainforests, ecosystems sustain biodiversity, regulate climate, and support all life on Earth.

What is an Ecosystem?

A Self-Sustaining Web of Life

Ecosystems can be found on different scales — from a small pond to entire biomes such as deserts or rainforests. They are vital for maintaining biodiversity, regulating climate, and providing services essential to human life.
